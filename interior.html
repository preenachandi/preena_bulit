Interior

At Builttech, we specialize in creating marvellous interiors, providing high-calibre fitouts that excel in precision and quality. We have an excellent team of designers who prioritize our client’s requirements, in terms of creativity and precision. The design team collaborates extensively with the engineering and operations team to offer top-notch interior designs that not only cater to the clients’ requirements but also exceeds their expectations in terms of visual appeal and quality engineering. That’s why Builttech won prestigious appreciation as one of the best interior designers in Kerala for high-end interior design and works for homes, offices, hotels, hospitals, malls, and resorts....
Builttech offers complete interior solutions right from rendering the designs to providing ergonomic furniture and state-of-the-art technology devices that complete the modern homes and offices. While keeping up with the modern elements of design and engineering, we also make sure to offer a touch of tradition and elegance as per the clients’ requirements.

FROM DESIGN TO MAINTENANCE

Our team comprises of accomplished interior designers, experienced project managers, artisans, carpenters, engineers, and technicians who are well-versed with setting up advanced fire-fighting, audio-visual, security, and other modern equipment. We work with the best materials that offer long-lasting and the best finish to the interior work. We also employ qualified and competent site engineers and supervisors who ensure that the work progresses as planned and gets completed on time.

TRADITIONAL KERALA THACHUSHASTRA

One of our specialities is our team of traditional vastu and thachushastra experts who specialize in traditional Kerala style of wood works for homes, offices and hotels. If you are looking to create a dream house in Kerala in the traditional “Nalukettu” style or are looking for the best interior design companies in Kerala to work on your dream hotel or resort projects with a traditional Kerala touch to it, Builttech is your perfect destination. We combine the latest technologies with typical Kerala’s style and culture to render style, elegance and finesse without compromising on your budget, quality and safety.
