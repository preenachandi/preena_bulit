Established in 2013 as a contracting firm, BuiltTech offers professional construction solutions. Since inception, we strive to attain precision and creativity that offer functional and aesthetic living and working spaces to our clients. We are a close-knit team of engineers, architects, and contractors empowered with technology. We aim to attain finesse with utmost quality, bringing a personality to your buildings.

We offer complete solutions right from designing, licencing and project approvals to completion and maintenance. Turnkey projects, residential construction, interior works and facades are our key competencies. We also undertake commercial and retail projects for construction, glass & steel claddings and interiors.
